How to Play

Fury of Anubis runs on a 6×5 grid with a scatter-pay system — no traditional paylines. Landing 8 or more matching symbols anywhere on the grid awards an instant cash prize. Every win triggers tumbling reels: winning symbols disappear and new ones fall in from above, potentially creating chain reactions.

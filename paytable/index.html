📋 How Fury of Anubis Pays
Fury of Anubis is a scatter-pay slot on a 5×6 grid (30 symbols). Symbols pay anywhere on screen — no paylines. You need 8 or more matching symbols to win. All wins are multiplied by the current tumble multiplier (starts at ×1 in base game, doubles with every winning tumble).

    How the Pay System Works

    Fury of Anubis uses a scatter pay system — symbols don't need to land on specific paylines. Instead, landing 8 or more matching symbols anywhere on the 5×6 grid awards a cash prize. More symbols = higher payout. After every win, symbols are removed, new ones tumble in, and the win multiplier doubles.

    ✕
    All pay values below are expressed as multiples of your total bet. On a $1 bet, a ×2.00 pay = $2.00. On a $10 bet, the same pay = $20.00. The win multiplier then applies on top of these base values.

    Symbol Pay Values

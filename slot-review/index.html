Overview

Fury of Anubis is Pragmatic Play's biggest release of 2026 — an Egyptian slot that doesn't settle for familiar ground. Rather than stacking wilds or chasing basic free spins, Pragmatic have built an entire win engine around a cascading multiplier system that can double ten times over in a single spin. The result is a game that feels genuinely tense, where a chain of four or five tumbles can transform a modest scatter win into something extraordinary.

At medium volatility with an RTP of 96.52%, it occupies a sweet spot that high-volatility hunters often overlook — frequent enough to keep sessions alive, explosive enough to threaten five-figure wins on the right spin.

Theme & Graphics

Anubis dominates the visual space — armoured, looming, and surrounded by green spectral energy that reinforces his role as the judge of the dead. The art direction commits fully to a dark Egyptian underworld aesthetic: stone obelisks glow with red hieroglyphic light, pyramids burn against a stormy sky, and the symbol set is built from scarabs, ankhs, eyes of Horus, and jewelled relics rather than generic card suits. It's one of the most visually cohesive Egyptian slots since Gates of Olympus defined what the genre could look like.

Gameplay Mechanics

The game runs on a 5×6 grid (5 rows, 6 reels) with a scatter-pay system — wins require 8 or more matching symbols to land anywhere on the grid, with larger clusters paying more. After each win, winning symbols are removed and new ones tumble down from above, potentially creating chain wins. Every tumble doubles the active win multiplier:

In free spins, the multiplier starts at ×8, ×16, ×32, or ×64 depending on how many scatters triggered the feature, and continues doubling from there. Combined with the gamble option pushing it to ×256, a chain of just three tumbles could see multipliers of ×2,048 or higher theoretically.

Max Win Analysis

The 10,000× max win requires a very specific chain of events: a 6-scatter trigger (or bonus buy to ×64), gambling to ×256, then achieving sufficient tumble chains in free spins. The math works out to approximately 1 in 4.84 million spins — rare but finite and documented.

💡
The Super Spin 2 (250×) offers the highest single-spin max win potential of any feature, entering with ×256 and capable of reaching ×1,024 in a single tumble chain — but costs 250× your bet for one spin.

Verdict

Fury of Anubis is a highly accomplished multiplier slot that earns its place among Pragmatic Play's best work. The DoubleMax mechanic creates genuinely exciting tumble chains, the Bonus Buy suite is one of the most complete in the genre, and the medium volatility makes it far more playable than its max-win potential might suggest. The gamble feature adds a layer of decision-making that most slots don't bother with, rewarding players who understand the risk/reward calculus.

If you enjoyed Gates of Olympus but found the volatility punishing, Fury of Anubis is the upgrade you've been waiting for.
